🌟 Deneb – The Bright Star ✨💫 


   Deneb is a huge, bright star in the night sky 🌌.
   
It is white-blue in color ⚪🔵 and one of the brightest stars we can see from Earth 🌙.

🌟Fun Fact


Deneb is part of the Summer Triangle 🌌, along with Vega and Altair.
    
This triangle is easy to spot in the sky during summer nights 🔺✨.

🌌 Quick Recap for Kids 


⚪🔵 White-blue supergiant star

🦢 Tail of the Cygnus (Swan) constellation

🌞 Over 200 times bigger than the Sun

🌌 Part of the Summer Triangle
