🌟 Betelgeuse – The Giant Red Star 🔴✨ 


   Betelgeuse (say it like “Beetle-juice”) is a huge, bright star in the sky 🌌.
It looks red or orange 🔴🟠 and is one of the brightest stars we can see at night. 🌙

Fun fact: 

In 2019, Betelgeuse got dimmer 😮
Scientists found out it was just covered by dust 🌫 — it didn’t explode… yet! 😉

🌌 Quick Recap for Kids 

🔴 Red supergiant star

🏹 In Orion’s shoulder

💥 Will explode one day as a supernova

🌞 Much bigger than the Sun
