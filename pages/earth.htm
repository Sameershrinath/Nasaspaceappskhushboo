🌍 Fun Facts About Our Earth 🌍


   Earth is the planet we live on 🏠.

It is the third planet from the Sun ☀ in our Solar System.

From space, Earth looks blue and green 🌊🌳 because:

Blue = oceans and water 💧

Green & brown = land, trees, and mountains 🌿🏔

It also has white clouds ☁ and polar ice ❄ at the North and South Poles.

Fun Facts


About 71% of Earth is water 🌊

Earth has one Moon 🌕

Earth is part of the Solar System with 7 other planets 🪐

Earth rotates on its axis 🌍💫 → This gives us day and night 🌞🌙

Earth orbits around the Sun ☀ → This gives us seasons 🌸🌞🍂❄

🌟 Quick Recap for Kids


  🌍 Our home planet

💧 71% water, 29% land 🌳

🌞 Day & night from rotation, seasons from orbit

🐾 Only planet with life

Earth is the only planet we know with life 🌱🐶🦋

It has air to breathe 🌬, water to drink 💧, and a comfortable temperature 🌡
