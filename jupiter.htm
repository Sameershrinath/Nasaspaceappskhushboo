🌟 Jupiter – The Giant Planet


   Biggest Planet 🪐
Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system. It’s so big that over 1,300 Earths could fit inside it!

A Gas Planet ☁
Jupiter is made mostly of gas like hydrogen and helium, so it doesn’t have a solid ground to stand on.

The Great Red Spot 🔴
There’s a huge storm on Jupiter called the Great Red Spot. It’s bigger than Earth and has been spinning for hundreds of years!

Lots of Moons 🌕
Jupiter has 92 moons! The four biggest moons are called Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.

Faint Rings 💍
Jupiter has rings, but they are very thin and hard to see.

Super Fast Spins ⚡
One day on Jupiter is only 10 hours because it spins so fast!

Long Year 🌞
Jupiter takes 12 Earth years to go all the way around the Sun.

💡 Fun Fact:

If you could float on Jupiter, you’d see giant storms, super fast winds, and colorful clouds everywhere!
  If you could float above Jupiter, you’d see huge storms, bright stripes, and swirling clouds. Some winds can blow 600 km/h—that’s super fast!
