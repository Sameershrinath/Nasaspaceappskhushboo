Planets of Our Solar System

Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet and closest to the Sun.

Venus

Venus has a thick atmosphere and is the hottest planet.

Earth

Our home planet, the only known place with life.

Mars

Mars is known as the Red Planet and may have once had water.

Jupiter

The largest planet in our solar system, known for its Great Red Spot and powerful storms.

Saturn

A giant planet famous for its stunning and extensive ring system.

Uranus

An ice giant that rotates on its side, giving it extreme seasons and a unique tilt.

Neptune

The farthest planet from the Sun, known for its deep blue color and supersonic winds.
